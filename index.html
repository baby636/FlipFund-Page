What is the purpose of our project?

The purpose of our project is aiming toward funding and helping students that want to start a new project. We would ask companies from all around Vietnam that are interested in angel investing for the future leaders of our country.

What are we aiming toward?

  • Provide funds for students in needs.
  • Connect students with companies that are willing to help.
  • Help students to organize their project neatly.
  • Lead these students' project to success.
